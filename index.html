Our Story

Neighbors Playing
for Neighbors

SODELO was founded in 2022 with a simple idea: bring orchestral music to Southern Delaware, performed by the community, for the community.

In just two seasons we've grown from 32 string players to a full orchestra of 75+ musicians — performing sold-out concerts and expanding outreach to local youth.
