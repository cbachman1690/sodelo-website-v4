Music Connects Our Community

SODELO is powered by passionate volunteers who share one belief: that orchestral music belongs to everyone. Whether you're a musician or a music lover, there's a place for you to give back.

Summer Program

Summer Sounds at Children's Beach

Summer Sounds is SODELO's music enrichment program bringing live orchestral performance and hands-on instrument exploration to children at Children's Beach in Sussex County. Volunteers help introduce the next generation to the joy of music — one note at a time.

As a Summer Sounds volunteer, you may assist with instrument demonstrations, guide children through interactive musical activities, help with setup and breakdown, and cheer on young musicians as they take their first steps with an instrument.

Chamber Music

SODELO Community Ensembles

SODELO organizes small chamber ensembles that bring live music into schools, senior living communities, libraries, and local events throughout Sussex County. These intimate performances extend our mission beyond the concert hall and into the heart of the community.

Ensemble volunteers are instrumentalists who want to share their talent in informal, meaningful settings. Trios, quartets, woodwind quintets, and other small groups are formed based on instrumentation and availability. No prior ensemble experience required — just a love of playing.

Commitment

Flexible · One or more engagements per season

Who

SODELO musicians and community instrumentalists
